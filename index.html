WHY?

Talents in companies like Apple and Google, have made more than one trillion dollars in market capitalization for their companies, but the majority of the market cap are owned by very few people, and not the ones who have created it. This is such a pity for those companies, but an opportunity for us to make history.

Chapter One, Foreword

Make talents benefit the most from the trillion dollar market cap

  • 1/1,000 of the $1 trillion market cap, is worth $1 billion, and 1/10,000 is worth $100 million, and 1/100,000 is worth $10 million.
  • Since one small portion can bring about such a huge amount of wealth, and $1 trillion market cap can make tens of thousands of people remarkably rich, then how about we use the wealth of a future "trillion dollar market cap" to motivate talents to create a new trillion dollar company?
  • To be precise, how about we use numerous 1/1000, 1/10000, and 1/100000 of the company, to attract and inspire tens of thousands of talents to start a business together, create a trillion dollar market cap company together, share the trillion dollar market cap together, and become "billionaires" together?

Chapter Two, Talent Shareholding Plan

Make the most "billionaires" of all time, with the most shares of all time

  • 1. The words "stock option" shouldn't just be a lovely term that company managers like to throw around now and then, but should play an important role in stimulating talents' enthusiasm for work. Therefore, the shares allocated to each talent cannot be so meager that it doesn't matter to the talent at all.
  • 2. Aomoxo Plan will distribute 90% of the company's stock among tens of thansands of talents, while making sure each talent owns enough shares that they have the potential to become a "billionaire", a dream worth fighting for.
  • 3. Aomoxo Plan is totally open and aboveboard with share distribution, everyone will always know exactly how much net worth they are fighting for: How much market cap can make their dreams of "billionaire" come true? How much will one's worth increase with each cilmb of the market value, $50 billions? $100 billions? $200 billions? $5000... ?

Chapter Three, A Brand New Company Structure

Running solely on the internet, it couldn't have been easier for talents worldwide to join Aomoxo

  • It's hard to imagine a more idealistic startup company. No office buildings, no office space, nor any kind of physical facility and equipment. Tens of thousands of talents will be working entirely through the internet.

Part Four, Changing the Rules

Zero Expense Model and Efficient Financing Plan

  • 1. Aomoxo will never use investors' money or borrow money to pay our stockholding members. Each dollar the talents get from Aomoxo, they must create first. Only when the company is making money, that everyone gets paid.
  • 2. Although it's an epic undertaking having tens of thousands of talents together working to create a $1 trillion market cap, Aomoxo will have zero office expense, and will become a highly frugal startup that can be up and running at close to zero cost.
  • 3. Aomoxo's Zero Expense Model has completely eradicated the root causes of problems like capital chain breakage. Our entrepreneurial process is capable of withstanding any storm that would come our way.
